ABOUT US

The Strathmore Providend Fund was formed in 1998.

It is a fund that caters for employees of four instititions affiliated to the Strathmore Education Registered Trustees (SERT), namely, Strathmore University, Strathmore School, Imara Educational Fund, and the Eastlands College of Technology
